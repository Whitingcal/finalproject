Content

Home page

Home Page: This site is for learning about how they can sign up for horse back riding lessons. They will be able to schedule lessons for themselves as well. Visitors will need to know their availability and when they want to take lessons as well as their budget. They will visit this site instead of others because it is the only place that offers riding lessons in the Rexburg area.

Images for the Home page

Sign up / Contact us

Visiters will be able to sign up for lessons. It will answer how much the cost of the lessons will be, how much it will be if you are renting a horse, how many days a week you can have lessons.

Images for the Page 2

About us

On this page visitors will be able to learn where the lessons take place, they can read about the location, about the horses they may get to ride and learn about thei instructor(s).
