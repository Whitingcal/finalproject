Saddle Up!

We made this ranch to provide anyone with the opportunity to learn how to ride a horse. Rexburg and the surounding area have little to no public options for horse riding lessons, yet we've notice a high demand for them. Our mission to change that, and we did! Saddle Up Horse Ranch provides high quality riding lessons at affordable rates. We love riding our horses and wanted to share our passion with others to be able to learn and know why we love it.
